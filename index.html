Our Mission

The Geraldine Blakey College Fund honors Geraldine Blakey’s love, wisdom, and encouragement. We provide financial support and mentorship to help all family members and family friends connected to Geraldine succeed in higher education.

Our Program

Guidance & Mentorship

Support with applications, essays, FAFSA, and adjusting to college life.

Scholarship

Yearly gift to help one or more family members or family friends take the next step in their education.
