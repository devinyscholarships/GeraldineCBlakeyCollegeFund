What Inspired the Creation of this Opportunity?

Devin Young created this program to give others more guidance and assistance than he had starting out.

He believes that a true legacy is defined by what you give, not what you gain.

Through this program, he aims to motivate and support future generations in pursuing their chosen paths.

Credibility

Achievement wise, Devin Young recently graduated from Bowie State University in just 3 years with a Bachelor of Science Degree in Information Technology, earning a 3.9 cumulative GPA.

In addition, his total gross income over the course of the 3 years that he was enrolled in higher education exceeded $50,000, allowing him to primarily depend on his own finances for every day life and all responsibilities that come with a means for transportation.

He did this all from his hometown (Temple Hills, MD)... without relying on a blueprint, fundraiser, academic/greek organization, or a middle man to obtain opportunities.

Two-Part Program

Guidance & Mentorship

Provides guidance and support with college selections, applications/essays, fiancial aid (FAFSA, scholarship/grant searches), adjusting to student life, and developing an individual professional presence/network while in college.

Scholarship

Offers annual financial awards to help qualified applicants pursue higher education.
