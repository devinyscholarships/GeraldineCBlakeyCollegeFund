Our Mission

The Geraldine Blakey College Fund was created to honor Geraldine Blakey’s love, wisdom, and encouragement. We provide both financial support and mentorship to help family members and family friends succeed in higher education.

Two-Part Program

Guidance & Mentorship

We offer guidance on applications, essays, FAFSA, and adjusting to student life.

Scholarship

Yearly financial awards to support college or trade school education.

Quote

“Education opens doors, and family helps us walk through them.” – Geraldine Blakey
